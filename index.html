The 7 day Daily Bible Reading Plan

Always remember to read your Bible daily

This will help you grow more and more spiritually everyday

  1. Job 1-14

    2. Learn about the story of Job, how his faith in God was tested by the enemy and how he overcomed. Later, God rewarded his faith and trust in Him by giving him ten times everything he had lost

  2. Rev 14-22

    3. Get to learn about the end times, what John the disciple of Jesus was shown and the things that are expected to come. Read and get to picture the events as if you were also present

  3. Joshua 1-12

    4. The way God called Joshua and promised to be with him. Joshua defeating all his enemies and conquering kingdoms. Also see how deceit depicts itself where survival was necessary

  4. Judges

    5. Learn about the judges of Israel, the people who were chosen by God to lead the Israelites before they asked for kings to rule them as other nations. Get to see a show of different great people, some who did extra-ordinary things

  5. Matthew

    6. Get to see about the story and life of Jesus on earth, the miracles he performed and all of His teachings. Know what God expects from us as His children

  6. 1 Samuel 1-25

    7. The story of Prophet Samuel, how miraculous his birth occured and how he became a great person in Israel. Also get to see the life of King Saul till it came a time that the Spirit of The Lord left him and a new king was appointed

  7. Acts

    8. See the life of the apostles after the ascension of Jesus, the way the Holy Spirit descended on them on the day of Pentecost and how the disciplles of Jesus did many wonders after
